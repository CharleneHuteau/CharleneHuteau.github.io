My name is Charlène, I am a French Freelance Translator working from English and Spanish into French.

I was born in France but the last 5 years I have been working and broadening my experience in different countries.

I have spent five months in Latvia and one year in Canada, both during my studies in order to improve my English and communication skills. Then I lived in UK where I could learn more about how business works in the translation industry working as a translation intern in an agency. I am now living in Spain for a year where I am working as a freelance translator while improving my Spanish language skills.

I obtained a Master's Degree in Translation at the Catholic University of the West, Angers (France).

My work

I am committed to provide high quality translations with a specific focus on food and textile industries.

I am specialised in Food and Care Labels, I have significant experience in the translation of lists of ingredients and textile fibres.

In this area I have translated a complete range of products and care labels by Paul Smith (32,000 words) and a range of drinks and food products for Costa Coffee (30,000 words).

 

I am also specialised in Products for Babies and Children.

I have translated different product descriptions, care labels and instruction manuals for babies and children such as strollers, toys, baby clothes, etc.

I have been working mainly for Mothercare and ELC (early learning centre).

 

I also translate General Marketing documents such as press releases, advertisements, brochures, etc.
